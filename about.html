About Qutaifan

We review genuinely free, open-source, and real-free-tier software — no hidden limits, no upgrade traps.

What we review

Tools you can use without paying first: open-source projects, local apps, and free plans with practical limits. A short trial requiring a credit card doesn't qualify.

How we test

  • Install or open the tool.
  • Check real pricing and free-tier limits.
  • Compare privacy and export options.
  • Flag limitations, don't bury them.

Why trust us

  • No paid rankings.
  • No hidden tracking links.
  • Updated when free tiers change.

Principles

Useful beats flashy — a small open-source tool with honest limits can outrank a famous paid product.
